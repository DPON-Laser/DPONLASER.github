## About DPON LASER

**DPON LASER（得邦激光）** was founded in **2005** and specializes in the research, development, manufacturing, and clinical application of advanced **medical laser systems**.

In **2018**, DPON LASER expanded its industrial presence by establishing a manufacturing base in **Anhui, China**, supporting the company’s long-term development in medical device manufacturing, technological innovation, talent development, and international collaboration.

DPON LASER focuses on the development of high-end medical laser technologies for modern healthcare, precision medicine, minimally invasive treatment, and clinical application scenarios across multiple medical departments.

---

## Company Overview

DPON LASER actively collaborates with domestic and international research institutions, medical experts, and engineering teams to develop next-generation medical laser technologies.

By combining clinical needs with engineering innovation, DPON LASER has developed a series of internationally advanced medical laser devices in China. The company continues to invest in core technology research, product development, clinical validation, and practical deployment in hospitals and medical institutions.

Today, DPON LASER is recognized as one of the medical laser companies in China with a broad product portfolio and extensive clinical application coverage.
