DPON LASER（得邦激光） 成立于2005年，专注于高端医用激光设备的研发、生产与临床应用解决方案。2018年，公司为推进全面产业布局，建立安徽生产基地，并持续加大在技术创新、人才储备以及国际化合作方面的投入。

公司长期聚焦企业战略发展与核心技术突破，积极引进海内外高层次人才，并多次与国内外科研院所及知名专家开展联合技术研发。结合医疗行业现状及精准医疗未来发展趋势，DPON LASER率先在国内研发出一系列具有国际先进水平的医用激光设备。

目前，公司已成为国内医用激光设备品类覆盖广、临床科室应用丰富的企业之一，产品广泛应用于十多个临床科室，涵盖：

* 血管介入
* 内镜介入
* 影像介入
* 妇儿系统
* 康复医疗

DPON LASER 已形成一批拥有自主核心技术的高端医疗产品体系，累计服务超过1000家医院，其中包括300余家三甲医院。多个产品与技术已在不同学科开展临床实验与技术攻关，持续推动精准医疗与微创治疗的发展。

依托丰富的临床案例与实战经验，公司致力于为全球医疗机构提供更加安全、高效、精准的激光医疗解决方案。

English Introduction

About Us

Website: https://dponlaser.com

DPON LASER was founded in 2005 and specializes in the research, development, manufacturing, and clinical application of advanced medical laser systems. In 2018, the company expanded its industrial presence by establishing a manufacturing base in Anhui, China, while continuing to strengthen its long-term strategy in technological innovation, talent development, and international collaboration.

DPON LASER actively collaborates with domestic and international research institutions, medical experts, and engineering teams to develop next-generation medical laser technologies. By aligning with the evolving needs of modern healthcare and precision medicine, the company has pioneered a series of internationally advanced medical laser devices in China.

Today, DPON LASER is recognized as one of the leading companies in China for the diversity of its medical laser product portfolio and the breadth of its clinical applications. Its products are used across more than ten clinical departments, including:

* Vascular Intervention
* Endoscopic Intervention
* Imaging-Guided Intervention
* Women’s & Pediatric Healthcare
* Rehabilitation Medicine

The company has developed multiple high-end products with proprietary core technologies and has served more than 1,000 hospitals, including over 300 top-tier Class III hospitals in China. DPON LASER has also participated in multidisciplinary clinical research and technical innovation projects, contributing to the advancement of minimally invasive treatment and precision medicine.

With extensive clinical experience and real-world deployment cases, DPON LASER is committed to delivering safe, efficient, and precise laser medical solutions to healthcare providers worldwide.
